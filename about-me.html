About Me

Hey there! I’m Siomarilis; yes, that’s a one-of a kind name, and just like it, my work is anything but ordinary. I’m a multidisciplinary artist based in Massachusetts, and I wear a lot of creative hats. My heart belongs to branding, but I can’t resist diving into the world of illustrations, zines, bookbinding, and even wheel throwing (yes, pottery too!). Whether I’m crafting sleek, professional designs or letting my whimsical side run wild, I love creating looks that feel both polished and full of personality. My toolkit includes Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Figma, Milanote, Visual Studio Code – and I’m always exploring new tools to keep things fresh. I adore experimenting with all kinds of mediums, but let’s be real – painting has a special place in my heart. The mess, the colors, the chaos… it’s the best kind of madness. If you’re into bold, creative expression, we’re going to get along just fine!
